«Sol Design Archive is an online collection of stuff with a focus on 20th century Portuguese graphic design and illustration but a voracious appetite for the full breadth of visual expression. The online archive currently features 613 images of 372 artifacts by 141 authors. Made for fun, out of love and to share by Ricardo Santos.»
