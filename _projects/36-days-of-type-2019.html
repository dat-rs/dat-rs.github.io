36 Days of Type is a project that invites designers, illustrators and graphic artists to express their particular interpretation of the letters and numbers of our alphabet.

In 2019, after some experimentation with the inaugural A, we started a series of collages following the formula B is for boom bap, C is for Camping, etc. This went on up to Q with an interruption on K. After that, we messed with other illustration styles, motion and some more collages.
