About

Office Santos is the independent graphic design and illustration studio of Ricardo Santos. We work in print and digital and our practice ranges from branding projects to illustration, editorial, typography, web design, motion and writing and research. We work with clients big and small on every time zone. You can reach out 24/7 for any inquiries, love letters, hate mail, collabs, and anything and everything in-between.
Let’s make cool stuff together!
